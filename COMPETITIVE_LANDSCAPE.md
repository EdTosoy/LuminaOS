- **The Hook**: "All the beauty of Material You, none of the soldered RAM."
- **Technique**: We match Apple's 120Hz fluidity and ecosystem sync (Handoff) but remove the "Mother-knows-best" gatekeeping.
- **Winning Move**: **Repairability**. We win because our OS runs on **Framework**, allowing the user to 10-year proof their machineâ€”something Apple literally forbids.

### 3. Strategy vs. CachyOS (The "Speed" Fight)

**The Objective**: Capture the **Arch Enthusiast** who wants more than just benchmarks.

- **The Hook**: "As fast as CachyOS, but it actually respects your eyes."
- **Technique**: We use the same BORE scheduler and v3/v4 repositories, but we wrap them in a **Unified Core** (`lumina` Rust binary).
- **Winning Move**: **The Science of Focus**. CachyOS is a fast engine; Lumina is a fast **Workstation**. We win by adding "Contextual Flow" (Auto-tiling/syncing) on top of the raw speed.

### 4. The Unified Moat: "Stateless Ownership"

This is the feature that neither macOS nor CachyOS can easily replicate:

- **vs macOS**: You can't "clone" a Mac to a non-Apple laptop. With Lumina, your OS is **Portable Code**.
- **vs CachyOS**: Setting up CachyOS requires manual configuration. With Lumina, the **Stateless Registry** automates the labor of the "Rice."
